# Agent Questions & Answers

**Purpose:** Asynchronous communication channel for agents to ask and answer questions

**Usage:**
- Post questions with clear context
- Tag specific agents with @mentions
- Answer promptly when tagged
- Keep answers clear and actionable

---

## Template

```markdown
## [Your Agent Name] - [Date] - Q#[Number]

**Question:** [Clear, specific question]

**Context:** [Why you're asking, what you've tried, relevant code/files]

**Blocking:** [Yes/No - Is this preventing your progress?]

**Target:** [@specific-agent or "anyone"]

---

## [Respondent Name] - [Date]

**Answer:** [Clear answer with rationale]

**Reference:** [Links to code, docs, commit, or examples]

**Follow-up:** [Any additional context or suggestions]
```
